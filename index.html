ANTHONY KUMASAKA

Prior to being a software engineer, I was the personal videographer and editor for Grammy nominated artist Ryan Leslie. While touring the world together, we spent a lot of time conceptualizing an application to grow the independent artist. This lead to the creation of SuperPhone – a bidirectional mobile communications application which would later be featured in Forbes(see link below). The creative process and battle of developing an idea into existence, and forging the bridge between conceptualization and a tangible, impactful product made me fall in love with programming. It is my outlet for problem-solving and creative expression. My technical skills and experiences include, but are not limited to JavaScript, React, Redux, Ruby, Rails, HTML, and CSS. Software Engineer experienced in React, Redux, Javascript, Rails, and PostgreSQL. Interested in building impactful products.
